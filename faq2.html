Q: What are Nutz and Boltz store hours?

A: Hours of operation are Mon-Fri: 7:00am to 7:00pm; Saturday: 8:00am to 6:00pm; and Sunday: 10:00am to 5:00pm.

Q: My order was damaged/wrong, what should I do?

A: If we made an error with your order, we sincerely apologize. Please call a Customer Service Representative at 1-800-443-501 and we will work to get it taken care of as quickly as possible.

Q: What is the status of my order?

A: Please log into your account to review your order status.

Q: How do I cancel or change my order?

A: We can cancel an order until it ships, our system does not allow for changes to an order once it is placed.

Q: How do I return an item?

A: You may return your purchase to Nutz and Boltz within 30 days. If you have a receipt, we will refund the amount of the item(s) in the same way as your purchase. Cash purchases will receive cash. Credit card purchases will be credited back to your credit card account.
